LEVEL EXPLANATION

LVL 1 - Top level players, if you meet them you have almost no chance to survive.

LVL 2 - Strong players, if you misjudge their strength, one such player can kill your entire squad.

LVL 3 - Players who are no less strong than the two categories above, but don't play smart.

LVL 4 - They are characterized by chaotic movement, but their shooting accuracy is below five percent.
